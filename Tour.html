The CCS office coordinates various student support services. This may include organizing workshops, seminars, and tutoring sessions to enhance students' academic skills and knowledge. They may also provide information on scholarships, internships, and career development opportunities relevant to the field of computer science. CCS OFFICE
The Cisco laboratory offers a simulated network environment that replicates real-world scenarios, enabling students to configure, troubleshoot, and manage network devices and protocols. It provides an immersive learning experience where students can gain practical skills in areas such as routing, switching, network security, and wireless networking CISCO LAB
The IT laboratory is equipped with a range of computers, servers, networking equipment, and software applications relevant to the field of information technology. These resources allow students to work on programming projects, software development, database management, system administration, and other IT-related tasks. IT LAB
The Linux laboratory is a specialized facility within the College of Computer Studies (CCS) that focuses on providing students with hands-on experience and practical training in the Linux operating system and related technologies. It is designed to support students in developing proficiency and expertise in working with Linux-based systems. LINUX LAB
